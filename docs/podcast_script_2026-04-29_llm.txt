The findings suggest a targeted probiotic approach may mitigate protein‑diet‑induced gut distress.

Probiotic supplementation during pregnancy can modestly improve vaginal microbiota and help clear common pathogens, according to a systematic review and meta‑analysis published in Acta obstetricia et gynecologica Scandinavica via PubMed.
